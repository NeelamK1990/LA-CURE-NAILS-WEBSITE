Welcome to LA Cure Nails

Located right off Interstate 95 EXIT (100B) on Biggs Highway North East, MD 21901. Our motive and mission are to ensure that our customers are happy when they come and satisfied with the service when they leave. We are passionate about doing nails and making everybody happy with what we do and make their nails and toes look beautiful as they want them.

All of our technicians are exprienced and has been doing this for over 10 years and are committed to make every effort to provide you with excellent service in beautiful environment at a reasonable price.

We understand that Safety and Sanitation are first thing that comes to your mind so be assured that our implements are medically sterilized and disinfected after each use.We use the most trusted brand products. Buffers and filers are used only once then discarded. All pedicure procedures are done with liner protection. And above all, staff are trained to follow a proper sanitization protocol that puts the client's hygiene as the number one priority. The art of beauty awaits for you come check out our skills!
